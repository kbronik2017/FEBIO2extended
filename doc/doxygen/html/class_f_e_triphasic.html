mat3ds FETriphasic::Stress ( FEMaterialPoint &  mp)

calculate stress at material point

The stress of a triphasic material is the sum of the fluid pressure and the elastic stress. Note that this function is declared in the base class so you do not have to reimplement it in a derived class, unless additional pressure terms are required.

tens4ds FETriphasic::Tangent ( FEMaterialPoint &  mp)

calculate tangent stiffness at material point

The tangent is the elastic tangent. Note that this function is declared in the base class, so you don't have to reimplement it unless additional tangent components are required.
